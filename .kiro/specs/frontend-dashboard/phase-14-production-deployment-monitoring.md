# Phase 14 (Optional): Production Deployment and Monitoring

[Back to Overview](./00-overview.md)

## Status

Optional and intentionally excluded from the current local-first execution path.

## Why deferred

The current product goal is algorithm research, paper trading, and backtesting on local data.
Production rollout is postponed until:

- strategy validation quality gates are stronger,
- operator/audit controls are deeper,
- live-risk controls are proven repeatedly in paper mode.

## Future work ideas

- Hardened deployment pipeline and release strategy
- Centralized logging/monitoring/alerting
- Secrets management and key rotation
- Incident response and rollback procedures

## Prerequisite before enabling

Phase 1-13 must be stable and reproducible on local-first workflow.
