# Strategy Spec Template

Use this template before implementing a new strategy. The goal is to make the hypothesis, market fit, risk model, and validation plan explicit before code exists.

## Required Sections

### Strategy Identity
### Hypothesis
### Market Regime
### Universe
### Timeframe
### Signal Stack
### Long Behavior
### Bearish Behavior
### Risk Model
### Exit Model
### Telemetry And Explainability
### Validation Plan
### Overfitting Risk Review
### Minimum Evidence Threshold

## Short Review Checklist

- Is the hypothesis economically plausible?
- Is the universe realistic for the intended account and venue constraints?
- Does bearish behavior match the actual product posture?
- Are fees, slippage, timeframe handling, and fill assumptions explicit?
- Is the validation plan strong enough to fail the idea honestly?
